HTML Beginner Questions & Answers

Beginner Level

  • Q1: What does HTML stand for?
    A: HyperText Markup Language.
  • Q2: What is the purpose of HTML?
    A: To structure web pages with text, images, and links.
  • Q3: What are HTML tags?
    A: Markup elements enclosed in angle brackets.
  • Q4: What does the <a> tag do?
    A: Creates hyperlinks.
  • Q5: What does the <img> tag do?
    A: Displays an image.
  • Q6: What is the <title> tag used for?
    A: Sets the page title in the browser tab.
  • Q7: What is the <h1> to <h6> tag?
    A: Heading tags with decreasing importance.
  • Q8: What is the <p> tag used for?
    A: Defines a paragraph.
  • Q9: What is the <br> tag?
    A: Inserts a line break.
  • Q10: What is the <ul> tag used for?
    A: Creates an unordered list.
